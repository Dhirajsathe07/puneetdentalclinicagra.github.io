We were the first - and for almost a decade, the only - dental clinic in Bangalore to use a surgical microscope for root canal treatment. All clinical procedures are done using the microscope thereby offering unparalleled precision. The surgical microscope helps us visualize and magnify structures up to 25 times their actual size. We can see and treat clinical problems which cannot be seen with the naked eye.

Advantages of Microscopic Endodontics:

  • Minimally invasive. Removes less tooth structure.
  • Allows clinician to see and treat root canal anatomy that is not visible to the naked eye.
  • Increased precision and predictability of procedure.
  • Photographs of each step of the root canal treatment can be taken and e-mailed to the patient.

We use digital dental radiography which minimizes x-ray radiation by 80%. Your radiographs (x-rays) are captured in a digital format and can be stored in your e-mail account. No more carrying your x-ray films around.


Photographs of the procedure are e-mailed to the patient. Overseas patients can forward the work done to their family dentist back home. This ensures total transparency in the treatment done.


We follow CDC guidelines recommended by the American Dental Association. In addition, we have a single-use policy on all files, burs and needles used during the root canal. When you walk into our clinic you can leave sterilization worries outside.


Our goal is to make our patients as comfortable as possible during their treatment. This includes providing you with a wide variety of music to choose from while you’re having your treatment done. If there’s anything in particular you’d like to listen to during your treatment, just let us know in advance and we would be happy to play it for you. We also provide orthopedic pillows that sooth your head and neck during treatment.
